About Me

I am a 2nd year MS student at the department of ECE at Georgia Tech. I work in the area of Machine Learning with Prof. Yao Xie. I am particularly interested in Machine learning for Dynamic Networks. I have recently been focusing on developing learning algorithms for Hawkes Process Network model.

In the past, I completed my BS degree in Tsinghua University where I worked with Prof. Shouyi Yin exploring deep learning for image detection. During my undergraduate, I also worked with Prof. Peter Beerel on Resilient Circuit Network Optimization at University of Southern California as a summer intern.
